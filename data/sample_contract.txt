SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT — ACME CLOUD SERVICES

1. The Provider guarantees system uptime of at least 99.9% measured monthly.
   Failure to meet this threshold incurs a penalty of $500 per hour of downtime
   below the guarantee.

2. Support tickets must receive a first response within 2 hours. Each late
   response is penalized at $250 per incident.

3. Physical hardware deliveries must complete within 48 hours of order
   confirmation. Late deliveries are penalized $100 per hour.
